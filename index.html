This is a map that marks global film and video texts that feature bodies of water. The map is a work-in-progress: more features are under development. It is a complementary cartographic guide to Ennuri Jo's book manuscript, Aqueous Image: Cinema and the Rhetoric of Water at the End of the World.

Based on Searchable Map Template by Derek Eder and Datamade
